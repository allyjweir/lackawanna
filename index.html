Lackawanna

Lackawanna is a Native American word for 'streams that fork'. This I felt was a fitting name for this project that takes in information and lets you divulge threads of meaning from the vast amount of media stored.

This project is a final year Honours project at the University of Glasgow in its early stages. Come back to find out how Lackawanna forms and comes together.
