What is a datapoint?

A datapoint represents a file or piece of data that you want to store within a research project you have in Lackawanna. This could be a video file, audio file, PDF, Word document, an image or basically any type of file.

Once it is uploaded you can annotate the datapoint, create transcripts, organise them into collections and add additional information to contextualise your data.
